The 18F Women in Tech & Data Hackathon + Training Day

November 7, 2014 9am - 5pm

Save the date! We are hosting a civic hackathon and training day at 18F (where is 18F?) for anyone who identifies as a woman and is interested in digital gov.

We will have awesome projects for everyone to work on, from experienced developers to total newbies. You can help out with a research project, build cool things with government open data, check out a new website and share feedback, attend training, participate in hands-on workshops, or just come to meet other cool women in digital gov.

Stay tuned. More details coming soon.
